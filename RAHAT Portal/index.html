Our Service

People living in Rural India still don’t know how to avail high quality or best in class healthcare facilities due to low literacy rate . Due to non accessibility to public health care and low quality of health care services, a majority of people in India turn to the local private health sector as their first choice of care. If we look at the health landscape of India 92 percent of health care visits are to private providers of which 70 percent is urban population. However, private health care is expensive, often unregulated and variable in quality.

Simplified UI

The Simplified UI is one of the main characteristic of RAHAT, so that user of every age group can use it without any hassle.Complexity free UI to ensure that even users with low literacy rate can be able to use to fulfill their healthcare needs. UI is the design of the user interface for websites or applications in order to facilitate usability and improve user experience.

Hospital's DataBase

The Database will contain the information about Hospitals facilities and Doctor available so that user can choose the medical facility and doctor’s as per their needs.The AI will be integrated in the Database which will recommend best medical facilities according to criteria selected by user.

Unique User ID

By creating a unique user ID , Users can keep track of all of their previous medical histories and healthcare information.This user ID also helps hospitals and healthcare officials to track patients and take necessary steps and precautions during the treatment.User-ID provides visibility into user activity in the environment and allows you to enforce least privilege – one of the fundamental security principles.



Patient Monitoring

By integrating patient monitoring system in RAHAT, hospital staff as well as healthcare officials can keep track of Patient’s medical history and treat him with necessary precautions.With the help of this hospitals can also have the user’s previous test reports all at one place.

Ambulance & HealthCare Services

They can provide the medical aid that is the need of the hour. The backbone of any ambulance service is the medical professionals who treat the patient first. They ensure to keep the vitals stable until they reach the hospital, and the doctors proceed with the treatment.Our Portal is the way through which they all can connect easily at one place.Get on-time HealthCare Services from nearest Healthcare centre.

Track all your Medical History

A patient's medical history can identify the chances of their probability of having lifestyle diseases like diabetes, heart attacks etc which are the main cause of serious health conditions. It helps doctors and care giver's to minutely assess and give the best of medical facilities to the patient. It will also be easy for Hospitals to track their patients mediacal history and offer them good discounts.
